    ABOUT US

    We at What and Why strive to make quality education affordable to the world. Over the years the cost of education has increased many folds to make it beyond the reach of many. We We want to remove this financial barrier to good education. So, we came up with this application to provide world class education to each and every individual who is interested in learning Programming Language. In fact, the only barrier to education must be an individuals thirst for knowledge. So as, What and Why is available to provide world classs Programming Learning. We are group os students who came up with this idea and implemented successfully.



    Our Vision

    Provide free Online tutorials which every individual would be able to access the tutorial globally from anythwere in the world, and is of Free of Cost.


    Our Mission

    So as to provide world class tutorial online, make all the programming available online and access across globally and learn programming languages.
